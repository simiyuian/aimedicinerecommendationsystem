Description

A viral infection of the upper respiratory tract that commonly affects the nose and throat. It is usually harmless and symptoms typically resolve within 7-10 days.

Precautions
  • Get plenty of rest and sleep
  • Stay hydrated by drinking lots of fluids
  • Avoid close contact with others
  • Wash hands frequently
  • Cover mouth when coughing
Medications
  • Acetaminophen for fever and pain
  • Ibuprofen for inflammation
  • Decongestants for nasal congestion
  • Throat lozenges for sore throat
  • Cough suppressants if needed
Recommended Workouts

  • Gentle stretching exercises
  • Deep breathing exercises
  • Avoid intense workouts until recovered
  • Yoga or meditation for stress relief
